About Psyche

Psyche is the first space mission to visit an asteroid made of metal. It is a protoplanet that suffered multiple violent impacts stripping away everything but its metal core. The asteroid is approximately the size of Massachusetts and has a surface area of 64,000 square miles. The core of Psyche is similar to Earths core and would be easier to examine than drilling to the middle of the Earth.
